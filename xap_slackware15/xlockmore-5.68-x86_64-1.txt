xlockmore (screen locker for X)

Patrick J. Naughton's xlock screensaver/locker for X, with many more modes added by David A. Bagley.

The 'Bob' bitmap has been included especially for Slackware, as well as a Linux penguin logo. (NOTE: the image of J. R. "Bob" Dobbs is a registered trademark of The Subgenius Foundation, used by permission)

xlockmore (экранный шкафчик для X)

Заставка Патрика Дж. Нотона xlock /шкафчик для X, с множеством дополнительных режимов, добавленных Дэвидом А. Бэгли.
