The KornShell language and interactive shell were designed byDavid G. Korn at AT&T Bell Laboratories. Besides offering a powerful interactive shell, it also offers a robust programming language with such features as associative arrays and built in integer math operations. ksh is an excellent choice for system administration scripts and for everyday interactive use.
